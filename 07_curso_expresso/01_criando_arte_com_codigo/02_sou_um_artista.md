## Caixa de ferramentas

```artista = turtle.Turtle()```

```artista.shape('turtle')```

```artista.forward(100)```

```artista.left(90)```

```artista.right(90)```

```artista.color('red')```

```artista.penup()```

```artista.pendown()```

```for _ in range(4):```

```turtle.mainloop()```
